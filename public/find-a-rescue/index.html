Find Trusted Dog Rescues in South Africa

Dog Rescue SA is a guidance platform — we don't house dogs or process adoptions ourselves. We connect you with partner rescue organisations. Visit them directly for current availability, requirements, and applications.

How it works: Each rescue sets its own adoption process, requirements, service area, and decisions. Dog Rescue SA does not manage applications or guarantee that a particular dog, match, or placement is available.
