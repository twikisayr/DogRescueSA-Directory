Find Trusted Dog Rescues in South Africa

Yes — you can find a rescue dog to adopt in South Africa through the breed-specific partner organisations listed below. Dog Rescue SA is a guidance platform — we don't house dogs or process adoptions ourselves.

How it works: Each rescue sets its own adoption process, requirements, service area, and decisions. Dog Rescue SA does not manage applications or guarantee that a particular dog, match, or placement is available.

Before you apply: most rescues will want to understand your home environment, other pets, and experience with the breed. Confirm the exact requirements directly with the rescue you contact.
