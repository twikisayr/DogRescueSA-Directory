Lost Your Dog?

We're sorry — losing a dog is stressful and frightening. The good news is that there are proven steps you can take, and a dedicated platform in South Africa that specialises in reuniting lost dogs with their owners.

What to do right now

  • Search your area immediately. Walk or drive through your neighbourhood. Call your dog's name. Check nearby parks, streets, and open spaces.
  • Alert your neighbours. Door-to-door or via your local WhatsApp group. Many dogs are found within a few streets of home.
  • Contact local vets and shelters. Call every vet and animal welfare organisation in your area. Someone may have handed your dog in.
  • Post on Lost Dogs SA. This is South Africa's dedicated lost-and-found dog platform. It's free to use and reaches a nationwide audience of people looking out for lost dogs.

Found a dog?

If you've found a dog, take them to a nearby vet to scan for a microchip — it's free and takes a few minutes. Post a found listing on Lost Dogs SA so the owner can find you. Keep the dog safe in the meantime; someone is likely searching for them.

Post a lost or found listing, search existing reports, and connect with a community of people helping reunite dogs with their families across South Africa.
