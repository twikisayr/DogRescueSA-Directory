Choose the pathway that best matches your dog. Rehoming help comes first; adoption information is available directly from each rescue partner.

Current coverage: This is a curated network directory, not a complete register of every South African rescue. The pathways below are the partners currently covered. Each rescue sets its own service area, capacity, requirements, and decisions. If you don't need a breed-specific match, our find a rescue page covers the same partners from an adoption-first view.
