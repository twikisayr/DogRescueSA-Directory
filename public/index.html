How this directory works

Dog Rescue SA is a guidance platform — not a shelter. We don't house dogs, and we don't process adoptions ourselves. We help route you to the right rescue pathway.

If you need to rehome your dog, we'll guide you to a breed-specific rescue that handles rehoming with care. If you're looking to adopt, we'll point you to rescues with dogs ready for new homes. Everything we do connects people with the trusted organisations that already do this work well.
