How this directory works

Dog Rescue SA is a signpost, not a shelter. We don't house dogs or process adoptions. Instead, we help route you to the right rescue pathway — connecting you with trusted, breed-specific rescue organisations across South Africa. Every link on this site leads to a partner rescue that handles adoption, fostering, and rehoming directly. We make it easier to find the right help, without duplicating what those rescues already do well.
