How this directory works

Dog Rescue SA is a guidance platform — not a shelter. We don't house dogs, and we don't process adoptions ourselves.

1

Choose your situation

Start with rehoming, adoption discovery, breed coverage, or lost-and-found help.

2

Find the right pathway

We point you towards a relevant rescue organisation or specialist support platform.

3

Continue with the rescue directly

Each rescue makes its own decisions and manages its own capacity and process.

!

Thinking about rehoming privately?

Before posting on Gumtree or Facebook, understand the risks. Public posts can expose your dog and personal details to people you cannot easily verify.
